## üìã Usage Guidelines

1. **Primary Crimson** (`#C41E3A`) should be used sparingly for CTAs and important elements
2. **White backgrounds** maintain a clean, premium feel
3. **Soft pink tints** add warmth without overwhelming
4. **Gray scale** provides excellent hierarchy and readability
5. **Shadows** should be subtle to maintain elegance
6. **Hover states** should provide clear feedback without being jarring
